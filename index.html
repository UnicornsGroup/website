Our Story

Founded in 2001, Suraj English Academy has been a beacon of quality education in Palanpur. What started as a small coaching center has grown into one of the most trusted institutions for English education in the region.

Our experienced faculty, modern teaching methods, and commitment to student success have helped thousands of students excel in their board examinations and build confidence in English communication.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality English education that builds strong foundations and prepares students for academic excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading English academy in Gujarat, recognized for transforming students into confident, articulate individuals.

Our Values

Excellence • Integrity • Student-Centric Approach • Continuous Improvement • Community Building
